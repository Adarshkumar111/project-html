SAVE EARTH | CLEAN EARTH

Avoid Harmful Gases: Minimize the use of substances that deplete the ozone layer, such as CFCs found in refrigerants and aerosol sprays.

Promote Sustainable Transportation: Reduce vehicle emissions by opting for public transport, cycling, or walking.

Support Environmental Policies: Advocate for and adhere to regulations like the Montreal Protocol, which aims to phase out ozone-depleting substances.

Educate and Raise Awareness: Inform others about the importance of the ozone layer and how to protect it.
