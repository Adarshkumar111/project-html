Sustainable

Sustainable development for vehicular pollution involves strategies and innovations aimed at reducing the environmental impact of vehicles.

Electrification of Vehicles: Transitioning to electric vehicles (EVs) to reduce reliance on fossil fuels and lower emissions.

Public Transport Enhancement: Improving public transportation systems to be more efficient and appealing, thus reducing the number of individual cars on the road.

Fuel Efficiency Standards: Implementing stricter fuel efficiency standards for vehicles to reduce fuel consumption and emissions

.

Alternative Fuels: Promoting the use of biofuels, hydrogen, and other cleaner alternatives to traditional petrol and diesel.
